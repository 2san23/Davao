Roxas Avenue Night Market

The Roxas Avenue Night Market in Davao City is a vibrant, daily street food and shopping hub open from 7 PM to 12 AM. Located along Roxas Avenue, it offers popular grilled items (tuna belly, isaw), local delicacies, and affordable clothing, attracting both locals and tourists with its lively atmosphere.

Key Highlights & Tips

  • Operating Hours: Vendors set up by 6 PM, with the best experience between 6 PM and 10 PM. The market runs daily.
  • Operating Hours: Vendors set up by 6 PM, with the best experience between 6 PM and 10 PM. The market runs daily.
  • Shopping: Features affordable clothing, shoes, bags, and local handicrafts.
  • Tips: Bring cash, as most vendors do not accept credit cards. Be prepared for large crowds and smoky conditions from grilling.
  • Location: Situated along Roxas Avenue, near Ateneo de Davao University and the Apo View Hotel.
