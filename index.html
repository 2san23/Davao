About the Province

Davao del Sur at a Glance

Davao del Sur is located in southeastern Mindanao, Philippines. It is known for the towering Mount Apo, agricultural communities, highland resorts, coastal getaways, and easy access to Davao City's urban attractions.

LocationSouthern Mindanao, bordered by Davao Gulf and nearby highland areas.
BackgroundA province shaped by indigenous heritage, migration, farming, trade, and eco-tourism.
Known AttractionsMount Apo, Philippine Eagle Center, Malagos, beaches, gardens, museums, and night markets.
CultureColorful festivals, traditional dances, crafts, cacao products, and warm community celebrations.

Tourists should visit for a balanced trip: nature adventures, relaxing coastal stops, family-friendly parks, food discoveries, and cultural experiences in one accessible destination.

Travel Tips

Before You Go

Best time to visitPlan outdoor trips during the drier months, usually from December to May.
TransportationUse buses, vans, jeepneys, taxis, ride-hailing apps, or arranged tours depending on your route.
Safety remindersCheck weather, follow local guides, wear proper footwear, and keep emergency contacts ready.
Respect cultureAsk before taking portraits, dress modestly in communities, and support local vendors.
Pack smartBring water, sun protection, rain gear, cash, insect repellent, and a reusable bag.
Book aheadReserve mountain climbs, resort stays, and guided activities early during festival season.
